Hiya's Sunniescollection is inspired by the fashionable culture around New York City and her personal interest in sunglasses. This prompted interaction with strangers through their accessory choices and through a series of questions Hiya categorised the data she collected to create filters depending on Location, Shape, where it is From, and Colour of the sunnies.






This is the main page of the collection where all the Sunnies are housed with a marquee running across the top YOU SEE A PAIR YOU LIKE?.

Here are the filters that will adjust the opacity of the image depending on if it fits the filter criteria or not.

When the image is hovered on, the border radius smoothes out to become an oval. The marquee motif is also present as you scroll through the page, this one says IT'S SUNNY IN NYC with a sunglasses emoji.

When it is clicked on, this pop up appears to provide more information regarding the Sunnies, with a back button that returns to the main page.
